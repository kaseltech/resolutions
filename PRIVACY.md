# Privacy Policy for YearVow

**Last updated: January 4, 2026**

## Overview

YearVow ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## Information We Collect

- **Account Information**: Email address and password when you create an account
- **User Content**: Resolutions and goals you create within the App
- **Usage Data**: Basic analytics about how you use the App

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the App
- To save and sync your resolutions across devices
- To improve the App experience

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted third-party service. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Data Retention

You can delete your account and associated data at any time. Upon account deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:
- **Supabase**: For authentication and data storage

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the App.
